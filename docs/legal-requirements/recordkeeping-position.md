## Position

The system should encourage strong internal records because they are useful for the family, the student, transfers, college, trade school, employment, military, personal archive, and later review.

The records are parent-owned. They are not routine MDE filings.

## Records to Support

- Curriculum and organized educational program evidence.
- Gradebooks.
- Progress reports.
- Report cards.
- Transcripts.
- Diplomas.
- Course descriptions.
- Test records.
- Portfolio artifacts.
- Graduation packets.
- Backup and archive exports.

## Product Wording

The app may say:

- "Create a parent-owned record."
- "Generate a family-issued transcript."
- "Export a graduation archive."
- "Your records show selected Michigan subject-area coverage."

The app must not say:

- "Submit records to MDE."
- "Certify compliance."
- "State-approved diploma."
- "Accredited transcript."
